ERP Education Management Solution

RESHAPING EDUCATION LANDSCAPE WITH TECHNOLOGY-ENABLED LMS
Due to a drastic transformation in the educational industry, many schools and universities are facing hardships in collaborating with students, teachers, non-staff, parents, etc. But with the utilization of cloud-based education ERP software, many have witnessed exceptional student-faculty experience, infrastructural growth, personnel management, centralized management, simplified report generation, and more.
  Tridots Tech ERPNext module supports your educational sector to manage student admission, timetable, attendance, fees, payment, examinations, grade books, online classes, video conferences, libraries, hostels, school calendars, events, transportation, custom email, and more. Automate daily operations and digitize the online learning experience on a single platform with zero coding skills.
